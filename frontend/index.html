How to Play

  1. The psychic gives a clue to help the guesser accurately place the needle to the target.
  2. Needle on target means SCORE!
    • 4 points for the center of the target.
    • 3 and 2 points for the outer portions of the target.
  3. If playing team mode, the opposing team can steal 1 point by guessing where the target is in relation to the needle (Left/Right).
  4. Enjoy the game!
